Winners Will See Their Character Come to Life!

Think you have the talent to design a character for Dark Solstice?

We are currently looking for character art submissions for possible use in our upcoming game Dark Solstice.

As a way to get the public involved, a total of four of the game's 16+ characters will have been designed by a non-employee.

Not only will the winners be immortalized in the game for their contribution, they will also receive royalties based on the number of players actively using their character.

Follow these Guidelines with the Design of your Character:

  • Dark Solstice takes place on a post-apocalyptic Earth, so give your characters a heroic yet burdened feel.Read More About The Game
  • For the members of the Awakening, we are looking for both female and male characters.
  • We are also interested in designs for intergallactic creatures/warriors.
  • Bonus points for characters that have a disability they've overcome.
  • No Nudity!
  • Nothing Sexually Explicit!
  • Keep Gore to a Minimum!
  • Beware of Copyrighted Material!
  • 3-D model file types should be emailed directly to admin@ravagedigital to prevent file upload errors.
  • Have fun and give your characters an interesting story!
  • Please submit as much information about your character and yourself as possible.
  • Each artist may submit up to three characters

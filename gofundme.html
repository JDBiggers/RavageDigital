New Company New Kind of Game Supported by the Public

Creating a game that offers new and exciting experiences is a costly and manpower consuming venture. As a new company in a technologically advanced market, we are heavily invested in the completion of Dark Solstice, not only for the gamers out there, but also for the children we hope to provide with much needed support.

As development continues we recognize the need to expand our staff of experienced developers, grow our hardware and cloud infrastructure, and develop other resources in order to meet our own stringent quality standards and ensure we meet our target date. This growth is a costly venture and we understand that the survival of this project will require an infusion of funds.

We want to offer our products and services at prices that are affordable and of the highest quality. In order to maintain these standards, particularly low pricing, we want to stay clear of any debt that would would force us to pass on unnecessary expenses to our customers.

It is for this reason that we are looking to the public to help us in exchange for early and premium access to the game with special characters, and other goodies for those who choose higher levels of support. For a minimal support amount of $5, you will gain access to Dark Shadows two weeks before it is available to the public and receive an un-lock code for two special characters.

Whether you are an avid gamer that wants to be a part of the next big game, or someone who feels the same way we do about helping children with disability, this is the project to get behind.

And, if you tell a friend (or two) to help support us, you will receive an extra 20,000 mech credits, which are very important within the game. All they need to do is enter your email address in the comments area of the support form and your access code for the game will be credited with the appropriate credits. Rack up your credits and be ahead of the game before you even start playing!

And upon successful completion of this funding campaign, we will also be delivering our first 1000 backpacks full of school supplies and goodies to children in need as part of our Packs of Love program.

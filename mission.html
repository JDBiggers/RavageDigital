About Us!

Corporate Responsibility

Ravage Digital is founded on the concept of delivering cutting-edge entertainment and business solutions with an underlying sense of corporate altruism as our defining factor. The concept of returning the support of our customers to local communities is central to our blueprint.

We believe that all actions taken by Ravage Digital should have a core goal of benefiting society and tackling issues experienced by children with disabilities and their families. Giving back is the cornerstone of our business model and drives our sense of ethical responsibility.

That is why a minimum of 50% of the company's profits will be returned to the community through aid to children's non-profits or through direct support to children with disability and families in need.

Our Platinum Rule

Always Strive to Give More

Because we are so dedicated to helping those in need, we want to take our corporate responsibility a step further by making a more specific and direct impact on the life of children in need.

We will make every effort to incentivize our products by including an extra level of giving that will have a more direct impact. Dark Solstice, our first gaming experience will be the flagship of this extra-giving effort.

For example, in the game there are a number of characters with some form af disabilty which they have overcome. When players earn credits using these characters, the players earn credits for use in the game, but each of the characters also has a virtual wallet where the combined credits are being saved.

Once a characters wallet reaches a certain goal, Ravage Digital will purchase and donate a specialized item to a child in need. For example, one of the characters, Enessa, has a prosthetic arm. When her wallet reaches a specified goal, we will work with the family of a child missing a limb and provide them with a premium custom-made prosthetic.

It is our goal to implement some kind of extra incentive such as this into as many of our products as we can, because we know it is only with the help of our customers that we can do what we do.

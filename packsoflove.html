About the Program

Packs of Love™ is one of our primary support initiatives aimed at giving children who have physical or psychological disabilty a little bit of happines in their daily life.

Many don't understand the challenges families of children with a disability face with events and expenses that most take for granted. For some, providing their children with even the most basic school supplies is an expense that is hard to bear.

While we can't provide every child in need with the necessary supplies and tools to be successful, we do want to help as many as we can.

At the completion of our first game, Dark Solstice, we plan to inititate our first annual Packs of Love campaign (help us finish on time!). We hope to be able to provide at least 1000 children with backbacks full of school supplies and other goodies the first year. AND WE ONLY WANT TO INCREASE THE NUMBER FROM THERE EACH YEAR!

We intend to complete Dark Solstice in the third quarter of 2020 and be able to provide our first recipients with their supplies before the holidays.
